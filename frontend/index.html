🔐 Assign Subscription Permissions

        
Grant Azure RBAC roles (Owner / Contributor / Reader) on one or more subscriptions to either team groups (team mode) or individual users (flat mode). The hack prefix is used to discover principals.


        

          

            
            
          

          

            
            
          

          

            
            
          

        


        

          
          
In team mode you can map team→subscription by listing one sub per team in order; otherwise all teams get all subs.

          
          

            
            
          

          
When mapped 1-to-1: Team 1 → Subscription 1, Team 2 → Subscription 2, etc. Otherwise all principals get all subscriptions.
